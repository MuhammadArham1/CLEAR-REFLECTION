THREE WAYS TO CHOOSE



We offer three simple buying options to give our clients greater flexibility and convenience. With 60 years combined experience, whichever option you choose, we can offer expert guidance, and most importantly, confidence, in choosing the perfect mirror.

Browse Our Collections




Choose from our latest collections including timeless classics and of the moment designs.

Our curated range of designs offers attractive options for every setting and a stunning focal point in your space. Choose from a variety of overmantels to suit a multitude of fireplaces, unusual shapes that make a statement in any setting. Round and oval mirrors are versatile and an effortless way to introduce softness in a room, as are light enhancing Verré Eglomise rectangles.

Make It Your Own - Customise an Existing Design




Subtle changes to the size and finish can transform the look of your mirror and the overall impact in your interior. We offer a wide range of size and finish options which allows you to easily customise your preferred design to suit your needs.

Adjust the size to suit the proportions of your space or customise the finish to get the look you want. Choose from finishes like traditional antiqued gold or silver, soft chalky finishes like French gesso or consider a champagne silver for a contemporary feel.
