Bespoke Design Service

Our uniquely personalised bespoke design service offers an experienced solution for our clients who are looking to create something truly special which is individually designed and made for them and their interior.

It All Starts With A Conversation

Whether you're looking for a feature piece, to maximise light or are struggling to find a design and finish that compliments your room, with over 60 years experience we can help.

Starting with an initial conversation we can discuss your specific requirements, guide you through the many choices with experience and understanding and ultimately help you find the style that’s right for you.

Home Design Consultations, In
Person Or Via Video Call

The most important outcome for us is that you have a mirror that you love and that works for your home.

Knowing how to choose the right scale, style and finish makes all the difference and that is why we offer home design consultations where we can assess your space and develop the design with you in situ.

There will be practical as well as stylistic options to consider. We will make suggestions that make your choice easy, giving you the confidence that your finished mirror will fit perfectly into your home.

Design Consultations are free and can take place in person, on site, or via video call, dependent on location.

Designing and Creating Your Mirror

In simple terms it’s a question of choosing the best style, size & finish. Understanding the criteria and effects of these choices is an art. We have gone through this process many thousands of times over decades which gives us a unique level of experience to guide you through the options.

Back in the showroom we will finalise the details of your design, and if the project requires, we can also provide computer generated projections of your mirror in situ.

As every bespoke design is handmade in our workshops, your frame is carefully created to sit in harmony with your interior.

We can also use fabrics and accessories as references when creating your mirror for a truly harmonious result.

A note on Colour Variations - Due to the handcrafted nature of our products, no two mirrors will be the same, all frame and samples should be used as a guide only, and your piece will be completely unique to you and your interior.
